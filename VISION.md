# OnyeAkuko 2026: Realistic, No-BS Quarterly Milestones

This document outlines the strategic roadmap for OnyeAkuko for the year 2026.

## Q1 2026 (Jan–Mar) – “Stop being a secret”
- **Jan 31**: Public beta live (clean landing page + working product).
- **Feb 28**: 500 registered users.
- **Mar 15**: First small media hit (TechCabal, Benjamin Dada, etc.).
- **Mar 31**: 1,000 total users + 200 monthly active.
- **X Followers Goal**: 800 (mostly daily updates and screenshots).

## Q2 2026 (Apr–Jun) – “People start recommending it”
- **Apr 30**: Basic mobile-friendly version (PWA / responsive fix).
- **May 31**: 2,500 total users.
- **Jun 15**: Launch paid tier (₦1,500–2,000/mo) → targeting 20–30 paying users.
- **Jun 30**: One solid partnership or integration (BudgIT, researchers, etc.).
- **X Followers Goal**: 1,800–2,200.

## Q3 2026 (Jul–Sep) – “Traction you can fundraise on”
- **Jul 31**: 5,000 total users.
- **Aug 31**: 100 paying users (₦150k–200k MRR).
- **Sep 15**: Regional expansion (Ghana/West Africa - 10-15 new sources).
- **Sep 30**: Raise ₦50–150M angel round or join an accelerator (CcHUB, Future Africa).
- **X Followers Goal**: 3,500–4,000.

## Q4 2026 (Oct–Dec) – “Clear product-market fit + real revenue”
- **Oct 31**: Mobile app beta (TestFlight / Android beta).
- **Nov 30**: 12,000–15,000 total users.
- **Dec 20**: 250–400 paying users (₦400k–800k MRR).
- **Dec 31**: Major recognition (TechCabal feature, 30-under-30, or MDA use).
- **X Followers Goal**: 6,000–8,000.
